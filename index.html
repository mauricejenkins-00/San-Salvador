Why San Salvador, Bahamas?

San Salvador, Bahamas has been a beautiful and tropical vacation destination, since 1964,
the same year the island gained its independence. This tourist attraction takes
you into a new tropical and sunny land of relaxation and celebration!

Facts for Tourist

  • The official language of the San Salvador is English

  • The islands land, and waters contain tons of wildlife

  • The Bahamas islands are on the edge of the Bermuda Triangle.

  • Nassau is the capital city
