Wild and Sea Life you might Encounter

These animals range from sharks to birds, to salamanders and wild hogs!

  • Fish: Hammerhead, Tiger, and Bull Sharks and Lion fish, jelly fish, and many species of crusteaceans.

  • Mammals: Dolphins, Wild Hogs, Seals, rodents and Manatees.

  • Reptiles: Lizards, Snakes, and "Trachemys terrapen" the only species of turtle in San Salvador.
  • Birds: Flamingos, Sea gulls, and a rare species of peacock.
